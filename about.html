AFC stands for Adam Fried Chicken. The name of this business was taken in conjunction with the name of the owner of this business, Muhammad Adam Haiqal Bin Ibrahim. In line with his wishes since being in school again where he became an entrepreneur and had a business in his own name.

VISION


AFC wants to be the best and number one fried chicken restaurant in Malaysia.


As we know, there are many fried chicken businesses from abroad that have indeed captivated the hearts of the locals. On that basis, we want to be the first local business to provide fried chicken that is able to compete with other businesses from overseas.

MISSION


The first mission is to increase sales revenue by 10% per month.


The second mission is to maintain good relationships with customers who can guarantee their loyalty to continue getting our products.


The third mission is to release a variety of new menus according to specific themes such as festive days.
